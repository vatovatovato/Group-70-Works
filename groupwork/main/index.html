About Us

Our history begins in 2022 when the goal oriented academy was first founded, the founder of this academy is Nika Keshelava. GOA soon gained popularity throughout Georgia, hundreds of thousands of children and parents became interested in this academy, and today this academy is the largest programming academy in Georgia with high-quality teaching and the best learning program in the world. Along with the growth of GOA, Nika Keshelava initiated various projects such as GOA Martial Arts (GOAMA) GOA Photography (GOAP) and many others, on January 21, 2025, GOA instruments (GOAI) joined GOA projects.This is a wide network of instruments in Georgia and a line through which the highest quality instruments entering Georgia pass, it is in GOAI that high-quality instruments enter Georgia and go to the market. Here you will find the highest quality instruments and various attributes, special instruments and copies such as Jimmy Page's guitar, Jon Lord's piano that he played with the band Deep Purple in 1970, you will find albums that only a dozen or a hundred people in the world have, special editions such as Dark Side Of The Moon (first released in 1960).
After the GOA group started working on a new project, GOA instruments, the main goal was to bring the highest quality instruments and accessories to Georgia. The idea for the project was born in 2023, and within 1 year the license, goods and other attributes were ready. When the project was launched on the market, it quickly gained influence, in 2 months the company had up to 100,000 users and the desire to buy new instruments was growing and growing. There were also difficult moments when competitors appeared to the company, but despite everything, things went so well that GOAI still took and maintained its place in the instrument market in Georgia. Soon after its appearance, it took first place in this market in terms of instruments and turned into a major center in the Georgian Instrument Market (GIS). By 2025, it occupied the number one place in the Georgian instrument market and became the main bridge in this field. The company soon went beyond the borders of the country and gained popularity all over the world. Today, GOAI networks are open in 34 countries around the world.
Today, the company continues to grow and develop, actively involved in various projects throughout Georgia and the world. Their main mission is to become the number one brand in the world in terms of instruments, their goal is to export Georgian instruments all over the world and introduce them to the whole world. The company continues to expand and plans to open its branches in all countries of the world, thus distributing the highest quality instruments all over the world. As we mentioned, the company is involved in many projects, they support many contemporary groups, are one of the main organizers of various concerts, and many Georgian and foreign groups perform at concerts with GOAI instruments. They also sponsor many companies, start-up groups or various projects, TV series or films, thanks to their sponsorship, many projects have been launched by various companies throughout Georgia. They also contribute to the development of the country and finance many public projects, transfer money to the Fund for the Poor, and are the main financiers in construction issues, helping many companies in this regard.
